Lasagna

Description

The best lasagna you will ever taste! It will make you never question lasagna ever again. (I mean, why would you in the first place?)

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown meat.
  2. Pour the jar of spaghetti sauce into the cooked meat.
  3. Rinse jar, fill ⅓ of it full of water, and pour that in with the meat as well.
  4. Add cottage cheese.
  5. Ladle a bit of the meat mixture in the bottom of a 9x13.
  6. Put a layer of noodles on top.
  7. Alternate noodles and meat mixture, but both start and end with the meat mixture.
  8. Add cheese on top.
  9. Leave overnight so the noodles can soak
  10. When ready to bake, cook at 350º for ~30 minutes, or until cheese is golden.
