Shrimp in Cream Sauce

Description

Want to eat like a fighter? Ripred the rat's favorite food is now easy for YOU to make! Taste the delicious goodness of this shrimp in cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 pound raw shrimp — peeled and deveined
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt (or ¼ teaspoon regular salt), divided
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 tablespoons heavy cream
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley)

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium‑high heat. Season the shrimp with half the salt and pepper. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until just cooked through — about 4 minutes. Transfer the shrimp to a plate, cover with foil, and set aside.
  2. Add the minced garlic to the same skillet. Sauté for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  3. Reduce heat to medium‑low. Add the heavy cream, cayenne pepper (if using), and the remaining salt and pepper. Bring to a gentle simmer, stirring continuously (about 1 minute).
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir gently until it melts and the sauce becomes smooth (about 30 seconds).
  5. Add the shrimp back to the skillet. Gently stir to coat them with the sauce and reheat — about 1 more minute. Do not overcook the shrimp.
  6. Sprinkle chopped parsley on top. Serve immediately — ideally over pasta or rice.
